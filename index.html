My Works

 Paul, in the Mind of the Criminal

Is a visual novel that combines elements from various video game genres and is a thrilling story that aims to solve a case involving a serial killer through the perspective of a detective. Although there are a lot of video games that are related to this niche, most of them have relatively simple storylines and are not considered to be detective novels. So, we have the opportunity to create a visual novel that is geared toward an audience that is not usually interested in detective novels.

Sigbjorn`s Odyssey

A project I and one of my colleagues worked on for roughly 5 months. In fact, this is a demo JRPG made with RPG Maker MZ which could attribute the Japanese characteristic to it. It follows the action-packed story of a shipwreck in which a Viking Prince and his companions were involved while trying to get back home to their kingdom before the King`s imminent death. Those features make this RPG seem almost nostalgic, yet the concept we have come up with brings forth a unique combination of narrative elements while being mostly inspired by Nordic folklore and indigenous cultures.

Meșteșugăreală

A faculty project which we created during our last year of study. Our site was used as a means of connecting artisans and craftsmen with potential customers and the interested public. We encounter great success while networking within the DYI artist community of Romania and we managed to effectively popularize less-known handmade objects producers.

Graphic Design Project

One of my first projects on which I worked this year in order to hone my skills. The goal of the project was to create a cohesive visual identity for the company, including a logo, business cards, and website. I worked closely with the client to ensure that their needs and vision were being met. The client was very pleased with the final results and I was proud to have been part of this project.
